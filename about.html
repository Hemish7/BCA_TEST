About Brandon Consulting

  • Established in 1996, Brandon Consulting has evolved into a highly reputable Information Technology solutions and Resource Management company
  • Over the past 25+ years, we have gained a reputation for quality, reliability and value among its clients across the US
  • With an eye on quality, we have attracted, recruited and retained exceptional talent from both local and overseas markets. Candidates undergo stringent screening processes to ensure the highest standards in quality, both technical and personal.
